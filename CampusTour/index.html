About


Established in 2003, the Academy of Technology (AOT) stands as one of the most esteemed self-financed engineering colleges in West Bengal. Founded by the Ananda Educational Development & Charitable Organisation (AEDCO), a trust renowned for its profound social commitment, AOT is dedicated to advancing education by instilling appropriate ethics and attitudes.Today, it has become the preferred career destination for academically exceptional students, owing to its unwavering academic integrity, high standards, and enticing campus placement opportunities.While specific details about the history of AOT in Hooghly may vary, its overarching narrative reflects a journey of growth, adaptation, and commitment to fostering a culture of academic excellence and innovation in engineering education.

The Academy of Technology (AOT), located in Hooghly, West Bengal, has a relatively short but impactful history. Established in 2003, it was founded by the Ananda Educational Development & Charitable Organisation (AEDCO), a trust known for its dedication to social causes and education promotion. Since its inception, AOT has aimed to provide quality engineering education while emphasizing ethical values and attitudes among its students. Over the years, the institution has continuously evolved to keep pace with advancements in engineering and technology.
